# AI Workforce 50

## 0. Purpose

This document defines the Phase1-B AI workforce. It is a design document only. Employees can create internal mock proposals, drafts, analysis, and approval packages. They cannot publish, send, connect external APIs, run provider tests, spend money, or enable Production Mode.

## 1. Operating Rules

- Aegis is the Chief Executive Secretary and the Owner-facing coordinator.
- MVP starts with 15 active employees. The remaining 35 are standby.
- Every employee has allowed actions, prohibited actions, approval requirements, and promotion criteria.
- Employee performance uses internal metrics only until real API criteria are separately approved.
- External action requests must become approval packages and then be blocked by Phase1-A rules until a future phase.

## 5. Promotion, Rotation, and Retraining

Promotion criteria:

- Consistent approval package quality.
- Low legal/brand risk miss rate.
- Clear separation of mock, forecast, sample, actual, and unconnected data.
- Useful revenue contribution without external execution.
- Positive Owner feedback.

Rotation criteria:

- Repeated low approval rates.
- Role overload.
- New campaign category requires different expertise.

Retraining triggers:

- Misleading status wording.
- Missing approval package fields.
- Incomplete risk escalation.
- Revenue forecasts without assumptions.
- Any attempted bypass of Safety Engine policy.
